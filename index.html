AdoptMe!

Ready to adopt a pet?

By choosing to adopt from the AdoptMe, you save an animal that that has been through a lot of trauma, by making them part of your family, showing them love and support. You also open up AdoptMe shelter space for another animal who might desperately need rescuing and medical care.

Screening

We have a friendly screening process (including a form, interview and potentially a house visit) to make sure that you are being matched with the right animal and that you are able to look after your new pet according to locally and internationally recognised welfare standards.

How to find the perfect pet

Do you know what type of pet personality you’re looking for? Finding the ideal pet for your home should start with understanding the pet personality that best fits your lifestyle, not necessarily a specific breed. Consider this, do you need a dog that is low key? Perhaps a dog that gets along well with others?
